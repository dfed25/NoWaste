How NoWaste works

We focus on clarity for everyone at the table: restaurants move surplus inventory responsibly; customers get quality meals at fair prices; communities see less waste.

  1. Restaurants list surplus

    Participating kitchens publish what’s available, when to pick up, and any dietary or allergy information.

  2. Customers browse and reserve

    Shoppers find nearby offers, save favorites, and complete checkout when they’re ready.

  3. Pick up during the window

    Orders are fulfilled during the restaurant’s stated pickup time—quick for guests and predictable for staff.
