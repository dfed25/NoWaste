Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

NoWaste (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates a marketplace platform that connects restaurants and customers. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services or visit this website.

1. Information we collect

  • Account and profile data — such as name, email, and phone number when you create or manage an account.
  • Transaction and order data — details needed to process reservations, payments, and pickups (for example, items, amounts, pickup windows).
  • Technical data — such as device type, browser, IP address, and cookies or similar technologies used to secure and improve our services.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the NoWaste marketplace;
  • Process payments and prevent fraud;
  • Communicate with you about orders, support, and important notices;
  • Comply with law and enforce our terms.

3. Sharing

We may share information with service providers who assist us (for example, payment processors, hosting, analytics), with restaurants involved in your orders as needed to fulfill pickups, and when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Your choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, or to object to certain processing. Contact us using the details on our Contact page to make a request.

5. Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and revise the “Last updated” date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the email and address listed on our Contact page.
